About SMART SCHEME

SMART SCHEME is a comprehensive platform designed to bridge the gap between Tamil Nadu government schemes and its citizens. We provide easy access to all government schemes available in Tamil Nadu, ensuring that every citizen can take advantage of the benefits they are entitled to.

Our Mission

To empower Tamil Nadu citizens by providing comprehensive information about government schemes available in our state.

Who We Serve

All Tamil Nadu citizens looking to benefit from government initiatives and programs available in our state.

What We Do

Providing accurate, up-to-date information and guidance on all available schemes for Tamil Nadu residents.
